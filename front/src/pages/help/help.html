Help

  Welcome to BookHub! This platform allows you to explore, manage, and enjoy a wide collection of books. Below are some key features and how you can make the most of them.

  To create a new account, go to the Register page and fill out the registration form with your details. Once registered, you can log in using your credentials on the Sign In page.

  Explore the latest additions to our collection by visiting the Latest Books page. Here you can find newly added books and see what's trending.

  Manage your personal library by visiting the My Books page. This section allows you to view and organize books you have marked as favorites.

  Stay updated with the latest news and announcements by checking out the News page. We regularly post updates and important information here.

  View your reading statistics and track your progress on the Stats page. This page provides insights into your reading habits and achievements.

  For detailed information about a specific book, head over to the Book Page. This section includes a comprehensive overview of the book's content, reviews, and ratings.

  If you need any assistance or have questions, please visit our Contact page. Our support team is always ready to help you.

  If you forget your password, you can reset it by going to the Forgot Password page. Follow the instructions to recover your account.

  To learn more about BookHub and our mission, visit the About page. Discover our journey and what drives us.
